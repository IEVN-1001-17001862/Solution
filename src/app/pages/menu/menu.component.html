Sanitary Wastewater

Our sanitary package wastewater treatment plant (WWTP) projects typically include the design and construction of the following components:

  • Lift station
  • Collection sewer drain lines
  • Flow Equalization
  • Primary and Secondary Treatment
  • Extended Aeration Basin
  • Sludge Aerobic Digestion and Holding
  • Sludge Dewatering (drying beds or filter bags)
  • Disinfection (sodium hypo chlorite chlorination, chloride tablets or chlorine gas)
  • Tertiary Treatment (automatic dual-media rapid filtering or sand filter beds)
  • Treated Water Recycling
  • Storage
  • Pressurizing
  • Distribution

Treatment is generally accomplished with an activated sludge process under an extended aeration format. However, our expertise includes other forms of treatment, including anaerobic or a combination of anaerobic and aerobic treatment.

Water recycling has become a key component in our WWTP projects to permit the reuse of treated water in bathroom toilets and urinals and in landscape irrigation. For manufacturing facilities under 5,000 employees (including all shifts), Solution Idea typically installs prefabricated steel-plate package units. For larger manufacturing plants, we normally build reinforced concrete treatment plants. However, our firm evaluates the specific needs and preferences of each client to customize the best solution for the particular case. Industrial Wastewater

    Solution Idea has the expertise to design and build process wastewater treatment plants for industrial pretreatment. These plants include sequential batch reactors (SBR). Treatment at our SBR plants involves pH adjustment, dissolved air flotation (DAF), coagulation and flocculation. The latter can be accomplished with the addition of aluminum sulfate and polymers (anionic and cationic) to produce the best effluent quality at the least possible capital and O&M cost.

    We have experience in evaluating existing treatment facilities by performing site surveys, inspections and assessments. Our efforts involve the review of plans, specifications and operational data of the existing treatment facilities in order to formulate, develop and implement a corrective action plan to bring plant effluents into compliance with governmental and clients' requirements. Our treatment expertise includes both aerobic and anaerobic digestion

Softened Water

    Solution Idea has deep knowledge in the design and construction of softened water treatment systems, primarily focused on industrial pretreatment applications. Our systems use advanced technologies to guarantee an effective reduction in water hardness, thus improving its quality.

    The core of our facilities lies in the use of ion exchangers and ion exchange resins. These components are responsible for selectively removing calcium and magnesium ions, mainly responsible for the hardness of the water, replacing them with sodium ions.

    Ion Exchangers: They use resins to exchange calcium and magnesium ions for sodium ions.

    • Ion Exchange Resins: Specific resins capture hard ions, releasing sodium ions.
    • Flow Equalization
    • Reverse Osmosis: Can remove certain minerals and contaminants.

Ultrapure Water

    The term "ultrapure water" refers to a type of water that has undergone rigorous purification processes to achieve extremely high levels of purity. This type of water is used in industries where the presence of impurities could significantly affect processes and products.

    The production of ultrapure water involves the combination of multiple processes to ensure exceptionally high quality, and its application is crucial in various areas where water purity is essential.
